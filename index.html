Welcome to BATCE Sixth Form's SGC

At Bishop Anstey High School & Trinity College East, we see sports as more than just activities; they're a chance to grow, make friends, and find your own strength, both in games and in life.

No matter if you're an experienced player, someone who enjoys sports on the weekends, or you're just starting out-you have a place here. Our club focuses on having fun, being fair, staying fit, and building connections. From exciting tournaments to activities that strengthen teamwork, we set up chances for every student to get involved, learn, and excel.

What we do

  • Arrange competitions between houses (like football, netball, volleyball, track & field, and more!)
  • Organize days for casual games to build skills
  • Encourage good sportsmanship, discipline, and healthy choices
  • Recognize effort just as much as wins

Upcoming Events

House Football Championship

Date: Janurary 15th - 23rd

Get ready for the biggest football showdown of the year! Intercup brings together all BATCE houses in a thrilling tournament full of fast passes, big saves, and unforgettable moments. It is not just about winning, it is about representing your house with pride, playing with heart, and cheering on your teammates.

BATCE Olympics

Date: February 2nd - 27th

Love sports but want to try something new? The BATCE Olympics is your chance! This fun filled festival features volleyball, dodgeball, tennis, relay races, and even a few surprise challenges. You can team up with your form class, your closest friends, or even mix students and teachers for a truly unique squad.

No need to be a pro, just bring your energy, and your love for friendly competition. Points add up across events, and the team with the best mix of skill and spirit takes home the Olympic Cup. Everyone plays, everyone belongs.

Video Games Day

Date: March 30th - April 3rd

Yes, gaming counts as a sport too! On Video Games Day, we turn the common room into a buzzing arena where you can test your skills in FIFA, Rocket League, Mario Kart, and more. Play solo or grab a friend for doubles you decide how you compete.

It is all about good vibes, fair play, and having a great time. We will have snacks, music, and plenty of chances to challenge your classmates (or maybe even your favorite teacher!). Whether you game every day or just once in a while, you will fit right in.
