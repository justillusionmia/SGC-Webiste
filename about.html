Welcome to the Heart of Student Sports & Spirit

Sports are more than simply games, in our opinion at Bishop Anstey High School & Trinity College East (BATCE); they are avenues for development, camaraderie, resiliency, and self-discovery. Our Sports & Games Club (SGC) is your home, regardless of your level of experience.

Our goal is to ensure that every student has a spot on the track, court, field, or even just cheering from the sidelines. Because being your best is more important at SGC than being the best.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive, energetic, and supportive environment where students can:

  • Have fun while staying active
  • Build character through teamwork and fair play
  • Grow both atlethic and personal skills
  • Celebrate effort as much as victory
